Best Swashed is a decorative serif typeface designed by Mans Greback, radiating bold charm and ornamental flair. Each letterform is crafted with striking swirls and curling terminals, giving the impression of vintage signage infused with playful elegance. The uppercase characters stand with dramatic presence, while the lowercase maintains harmony with refined, flowing details.  

Its unique personality comes from the fusion of classic serif structure with elaborate embellishments, making it both readable and visually captivating. From lavish headlines to expressive branding, Best Swashed delivers artistry in every curve.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers. 

Mans Greback is known for crafting high-quality, expressive typefaces that blend artistic creativity with technical precision, ensuring each font is both functional and visually distinctive.
